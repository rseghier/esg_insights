#SolarPower Shines Light on #AfricanEconomy
Key Takeaways:
- Access to reliable energy is critical for African businesses.
- Solar PV installations combat Africa's energy poverty.
- Decentralized solar power prompts an economic revolution in Africa.

Further Insights
1. Energy Poverty:
43% of Africans lack access to reliable electricity, severely impacting economic growth and quality of life.

2. Economic Impact:
Reliable power could have doubled Africa's GDP growth rate per person from 1995 to 2007.

3. Solar Solution:
Record solar PV capacity installed in Africa last year, with 65% from private firms directly contracting with developers.
