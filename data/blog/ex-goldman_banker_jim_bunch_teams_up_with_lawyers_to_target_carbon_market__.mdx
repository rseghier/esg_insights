Key Takeaways:
- Transition from carbon credits to focusing on reducing actual supply chain emissions.
- STACs offer a direct transaction to reward suppliers for meeting emissions reduction milestones.
- STACs enhance financial instruments for decarbonizing supply chains, moving beyond traditional carbon credits.

Further Insights

Jim Bunch, alongside Linklaters and Scope 3 Climate Capital, introduces the sector-transition acceleration contract (STAC) as an alternative to conventional carbon credits, aiming to directly reduce supply chain emissions.
