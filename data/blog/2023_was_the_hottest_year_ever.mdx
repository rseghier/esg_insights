Key Takeaways:
- Last year, the globe hit its highest recorded temperature.
- Human emissions main culprit, with El Niño's amplification.
- Next year may be even hotter due to El Niño.

Further Insights
1. Global Warming:
2023 set a new global temperature record at 1.48°C above pre-industrial levels.

2. Human Impact:
Human greenhouse-gas emissions are identified as the primary cause for the increase in global temperatures.

3. El Niño Effect:
The warming effect was further amplified by El Niño, a natural climate phenomenon, suggesting a potential trend.
