#PwC Survey Boosts #Investor #Sustainability Focus
Key Takeaways:
- Investors prioritize impact, seeing it as an indicator of long-term prospects.
- Sustainability and emerging tech are key to resilience and transformation.
- Investors demand credible sustainability reporting and action plans.

Further Insights
1. Global Investor Survey:
PwC's Global Investor Survey reveals that investors are keen on understanding how companies manage crises, with a focus on sustainability and emerging technology.

2. Investor Insights:
Investors are more likely to invest in companies that can credibly address their negative impacts on the environment and society.

3. Reporting Challenges:
Companies face challenges in sustainability reporting, including data collection and aligning with business strategy.

4. Investment Influence:
A company's sustainability efforts and their ability to manage long-term risks and opportunities significantly influence investment decisions.

5. Regulatory Implications:
Future regulations may be influenced by a company's current impact, highlighting the importance of sound sustainability strategies.

6. Greenwashing Concern:
94% of investors feel that there are unsupported claims in sustainability reporting, indicating a need for more credible disclosures.

Closing Thoughts:
Given the urgent need for credible sustainability reporting and its impact on investment decisions, how can companies better align their reporting practices with investor expectations and regulatory requirements?
