Driving #GenderEquality Through #Sustainability at #SchneiderElectric
Key Takeaways:
- Schneider Electric merges sustainability with progress, treating it as a core strategy.
- Gender equality is central to Schneider Electric's action plan, with precise inclusivity goals.
- Schneider Electric's targets for hiring and leadership roles aim to significantly boost gender diversity.

Further Insights
1. Core Strategy:
Schneider Electric sees sustainability not just as a responsibility but as a significant opportunity to combat climate change and make a substantial impact.

2. Gender Equality:
The company has set ambitious targets to ensure gender parity: 50% of new hires to be women, 40% front-line managers, and 30% senior leadership roles.

3. Industry Engagement:
Participating in the Bloomberg Sustainable Business Summit showcases Schneider Electric's commitment to sustainability and provides a platform to share and learn best practices.

Closing Thoughts:
As Schneider Electric leads with its sustainability and gender equality agenda, how can other firms adapt such models to reinforce their own ESG commitments?
