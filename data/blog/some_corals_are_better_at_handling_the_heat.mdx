Coral Resilience Amid #ClimateChange #MarineBiodiversity
Key Takeaways:
- Some corals can withstand higher temperatures, showing resilience.
- Rapid global warming outpaces natural selection for coral resilience.
- Human activity is the main factor accelerating global temperatures.

Further Insights
1. Coral Threat:
Coral reefs, hosting a quarter of marine species, are endangered by rising water temperatures causing bleaching.

2. Resilience Variability:
Corals in the Red Sea exhibit higher temperature tolerance due to millennia of adaptation, highlighting resilience variability.

3. Human Impact:
The accelerated pace of global warming due to human activity challenges the natural resilience-building process of corals.

Closing Thoughts:
