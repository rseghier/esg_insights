Navigating #GHGReporting Challenges & Solutions
Key Takeaways:
- Understanding scopes critical for accurate GHG emission reporting.
- Organizational and operational boundaries define reporting structures.
- Materiality and control mentality key for data integrity.

Further Insights
1. GHG Emissions:
Companies globally are preparing mandatory greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions reporting, moving from voluntary to required frameworks.

2. Reporting Landscape:
The dynamic sustainability reporting landscape presents practical difficulties, notably around the judgment and challenges in calculating GHG emissions.

3. Emission Scopes:
Emissions are categorized into Scopes 1 (direct), 2 (indirect from purchased energy), and 3 (all other indirect) crucial for thorough reporting.

Closing Thoughts:
As the regulatory environment tightens around GHG reporting, how prepared are companies in adapting their policies and controls to ensure accuracy and integrity of their sustainability data?
