Empowering #GRESB #RealEstate #Sustainability Insights
Key Takeaways:
- GRESB's Real Benchmarks tool enhances portfolio energy and GHG insights.
- Asset Impact aids financial institutions in climate action and reporting.
- The Carbon Footprint Dashboard visualizes portfolio carbon data effectively.

Further Insights
1. GRESB Introduction:
GRES offers updates on new tools for actionable insights into the energy and GHG performance of real estate assets.

2. Real Statistics:
Provides unique energy and GHG emission intensities for calculating financed emissions, leveraging a database of over 170,000 real estate assets.

3. Asset Impact:
Enables reporting on financed emissions and climate action through asset-based data solutions, excluding real estate.

4. Carbon Footprint Dashboard:
Offers institutional investors tools to explore and visualize energy use and GHG emissions data by geography and sector.

5. GRESB Standards & Tools:
Updates to the GRESB standards and the introduction of new tools, like the carbon footprint tool report, aim to support informed investment decisions.
