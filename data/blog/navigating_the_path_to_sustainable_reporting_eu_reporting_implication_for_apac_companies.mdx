Decoding #EUReporting for #APAC Companies #Sustainability
Key Takeaways:
- Understanding EU reporting implications is critical for APAC companies.
- Scope 3 emissions data transparency is a major requirement under ESRS.
- Start sustainability reporting early to navigate compliance smoothly.

Further Insights
1. EU Reporting Mandates:
The EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) mandates detailed sustainability disclosures, impacting both directly and indirectly associated APAC companies.

2. Materiality & Double Materiality:
Materiality assessments under CSRD require companies to report both the impact of the environment on their operations and their impact on the environment.

3. Data Accuracy & Scope 3:
Emphasis on accurate and transparent reporting of Scope 3 emissions, requiring primary data from value chain partners.

Closing Thoughts:
As the landscape of sustainability reporting evolves with stringent regulations like the CSRD, how prepared is your business to adapt and thrive in this new era?
