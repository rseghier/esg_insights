Vietnam is witnessing a substantial increase in sustainable initiatives across sectors, with real estate at the forefront. The integration of ESG considerations attracts investors and consumers alike.

Government policies in Vietnam promote green energy and waste management, aiming to reduce environmental degradation and enhance corporate ESG standards.

3. GRESB Integration:
GRESB serves as an ESG report card for real estate, emphasizing the importance of Green Building certifications and sustainability frameworks in the assessment process.

Sustainability-linked loans and investments are increasingly influenced by ESG benchmarks like GRESB, offering financial incentives for sustainable development practices.
