Convergence Approach
Industry-wide emission intensity targets account for starting points, driving equitable, technology-neutral decarbonization across the sector.

Core Emission Sources
Inclusion of core boundaries (from power production to hot rolling) ensures critical, material emission sources are accounted for, enabling focused reduction efforts irrespective of asset ownership.

Scrap Utilization
The methodology accounts for increased scrap availability, encouraging efficient resource use and acknowledging its role in reducing sectoral emissions.

Upstream Methane Emissions
While currently outside the core boundary due to data robustness, Upstream methane emissions are emphasized for their importance in comprehensively addressing climate impact.

Technology Neutrality
The guidance underscores the role of technology neutrality, ensuring companies can choose the most effective routes to decarbonization based on their unique circumstances and capabilities.
