Defining #ZeroEmissions in #Buildings: #Biden's Vision
Key Takeaways:
- Establishing a standard industry definition for zero-emission buildings.
- Creating a guide for top-tier building energy performance in the US.
- Sending a strong market signal about the future of sustainable building.

Further Insights
1. New Definition:
The Biden administration introduces a federal definition of zero-emissions buildings, aiming to standardize the term across the industry.

2. Market Signal:
Though the guidance has no legal weight, it is intended as a robust market signal to encourage advancements in sustainable building practices.

3. Education and Training:
The initiative is demonstrated through practical projects, like Requity's training program in West Baltimore, showing direct community and educational benefits.

Closing Thoughts:
As the definition of zero-emission buildings gains clarity, will this spark a new era in sustainable construction practices and how will the market react to these standards?
