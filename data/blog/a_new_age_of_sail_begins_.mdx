#FlettnerRotors: Revolutionizing #Maritime #Sustainability
Key Takeaways:
- Flettner rotors reduce ship fuel consumption by over half.
- Reviving old technologies can address modern sustainability challenges.
- Maritime industry needs viable options to meet 2050 net-zero targets.

Further Insights
1. Innovative Arrival:
In 1926, the converted ship Baden-Baden showcased Flettner rotors, demonstrating significant fuel savings and efficiency.

2. Sustainability Demand:
As oil costs—both financial and environmental—rise, the shipping industry is under pressure to find sustainable alternatives.

3. Emissions Challenge:
Shipping is responsible for 3% of global emissions, prompting ports and organizations to set strict emission limits.

Closing Thoughts:
Could revisiting and improving upon forgotten technologies like Flettner rotors be the key to unlocking sustainability in industries resistant to change? Let's discuss potential pathways and innovations that could guide the maritime sector towards a greener future.
